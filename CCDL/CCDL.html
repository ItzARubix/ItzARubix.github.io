Foreverred Trueskip (Lunar Heart)

Placement: 1

Requirements:

  • Complete Foreverred’s Trueskip route by progressing to the right after opening the chest
  • Reach the end of the game without dying
  • You do not need to collect any optional collectables

Infinite Hall of Pain

Placement: 2

Requirements:

  • Complete the first room of Infinite Hall of Pain from start to finish without dying
  • You do not need to complete any other parts of the map

Everred Trueskip (Moon Reddish)
