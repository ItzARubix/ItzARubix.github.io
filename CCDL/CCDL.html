1: Foreverred Trueskip (Lunar Heart)

Requirements:

  • Complete Foreverred's Trueskip route by progressing to the right after opening the chest
  • Reach True Summit and collect the Lunar Heart without dying
  • You do not need to collect any other optional collectables
